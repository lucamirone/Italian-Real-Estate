About / Advisory Approach

The best Italian property search begins before the property search.

It begins with understanding the buyer. We use strategic buyer profiling to clarify goals, constraints, motivations, and decision criteria before opportunities are evaluated.

Advisory philosophy

American buyers often arrive with a region, a dream property, or a beautiful listing. Our role is to slow the first decision down enough to understand lifestyle fit, intended use, risk tolerance, professional needs, and whether the search is aligned with the realities of the Italian market.

  • Understand the buyer's goals
  • Build a detailed buyer profile
  • Identify suitable regions and property types
  • Clarify intended use: lifestyle, relocation, income, legacy, tax planning, or investment
  • Coordinate next steps with appropriate licensed professionals
  • Help avoid confusion, emotional decisions, and poorly matched opportunities

Italian Market Opportunity

Why American buyers look at Italy.

Italy attracts U.S. buyers for lifestyle, culture, historic towns and countryside, coastal and lake destinations, food, wine, art, architecture, design, family legacy, and European living.

For some buyers, Italian real estate also offers relative value compared with certain U.S. luxury markets. For others, the appeal is less financial and more personal: authentic, one-of-a-kind properties with character, place, and long-term meaning.

Lifestyle and culture Historic towns and countryside Coastal and lake destinations Food, wine, art, architecture, and design Family legacy and European lifestyle Authentic, rare, one-of-a-kind properties

Ultra-High-Net-Worth Buyers

For ultra-high-net-worth individuals, the provided analysis identifies the €200,000 annual flat tax as the only specific tax-related cost mentioned. Other legal, administrative, ownership, inheritance, or operational costs should be independently verified with qualified professionals.

Buyers in this segment may consider property budgets exceeding €2 million to €3 million and may prioritize uniqueness, authenticity, privacy, architectural significance, location, and lifestyle value over standard return-on-investment calculations.
